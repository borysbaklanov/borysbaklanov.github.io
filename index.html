I find the greatest joy in creating things together with other people. Things that benefit people and solve their needs. Things that bring joy to the customers and money to the creators. Good things.

I’m a software designer, building products that are useful, simple and fun. I love working with enthusiastic people on problems that I could relate to. In my spare time, I enjoy things that bring something new to my work: video & board games, art history, human psychology, photography, and pop culture. I’m also a huge fan of snacks.
I live in Berlin, so if you’re ever here — let’s meet for a cup of tea.

What you can expect working with me

  • I am a generalist, designing from an idea to a released product & beyond
  • I consider data analysis, research and prototyping to be my strong suits
  • I care a lot about evoking emotions with my design
  • I oppose unethical design decisions
  • I work really fast
  • I keep focus and act on what brings the most value
  • I prefer to work async and hold meetings exclusively for decision making

Reach me out by borys.baklanov@gmail.com
I’m always happy to chat.
